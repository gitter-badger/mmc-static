Irish Impact Conference


Register Now!

October 29-30, 2015

Impact investing is the topic of the 2015 Irish Impact Conference, and it is something Andrea (Andi) Phillips takes quite seriously. Phillips, who will be the keynote speaker at the conference, is the vice president for Goldman Sachs' (GS) Urban Investment Group. She leads the firm's Social Impact Fund and deal teams, focusing on social impact bonds and economic development financing. Phillips also developed the Access to Capital program as part of the 10,000 Small Businesses Initiative, and led the investment in the Rikers Island Social Impact Bond, the first such transaction ever executed by a financial institution in the U.S. market.

Register now and join fellow practitioners from around the nation as they come to Notre Dame to learn from Andi Phillips. The Irish Impact Conference provides you with a great opportunity to network with fellow impact investors to discuss the importance of investing in efforts that not only provide a return on investment, but also target specific social and/or environmental needs.

In the meantime, be sure to visit IrishImpact.nd.edu where we will continue to post additional information about the Irish Impact Conference. And don’t miss our blog at IrishImpact.wordpress.com!

I look forward to seeing you!
