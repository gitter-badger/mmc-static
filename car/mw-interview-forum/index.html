About the Midwest MBA Interview Forum
The Midwest MBA Interview Forum is a premier invitation-only interview event that connects qualified, diversified MBA candidates with the top employers globally. With a focus on the most talented first-year MBA candidates, employers will have the opportunity to fill full-time roles in the United States and abroad.

Interview MBA candidates from these top schools

What to expect as a recruiter:

  • Access to MBA talent from highly ranked graduate business programs
  • More than 500 diversified and qualified MBA candidates who are prepared for full-time employment and intern opportunities
  • A focus on first-year MBA talent, plus opportunities to fill full-time roles.
