What can the Notre Dame Executive MBA program offer you? Success. How you define success, whether personal or professional, will determine whether the University of Notre Dame is right for you.

For now, here are three key takeaways of our Executive MBA program for you to consider:

  1. You become a different kind of leader — a leader for the greater good
    Notre Dame's approach to leadership is taught on a strategic, holistic level, and it's an approach to business that teaches you how to think differently, lead with your values, and assess the impact of your decisions.


  2. You don't just earn a degree, you join a team and a community
    One of the first things you do in the Executive MBA program is join a small study team that may include engineers, attorneys, physicians and entrepreneurs. Together, you draw on each other's expertise, experience and wisdom as you develop into stronger leaders.


  3. You will forever align yourself with one of the most storied universities in the world

Now that you've seen the impact the Notre Dame EMBA program has had on some of our alums, take a moment to envision yourself in the program. The best way to learn how the Executive MBA can impact your life and your career is by attending an event.
