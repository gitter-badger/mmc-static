You want to take the next step in your development as a leader, but one question comes up every time you consider making the leap: 'How can I make graduate school fit into my life?'

Notre Dame has the answers: two Executive MBA formats in two different cities. Both allow you to stay home during the week, so you only step away from work and family on the weekends.

Immerse yourself in the historic Notre Dame Campus

  • Meet one weekend per month (Thurs. through Sat.) at the Stayer Center for Executive Education
  • Worried about making it to campus once a month? We have students from all over the country, including California, Florida and New Jersey, who make the trip each month
  • The once-per-month format allows you more time to study AND time to balance work and family time
  • Begin in August and complete you degree in 22 months
  • Gain a global business perspective through your international business immersion project in January

Accelerate your Executive MBA experience in the heart of downtown Chicago

  • Complete your degree in only 18 months (Jan. orientation — May graduation)
  • Meet on alternating weekends (Fri.-Sat.) on Notre Dame's Chicago campus located in the Loop on Michigan Avenue
  • Deepen your ND experience during two weeklong immersions on the campus of Notre Dame
  • And, new this year for the Chicago cohort, join your South Bend classmates for your international business immersion project in January

Why not join me for an information session where you can learn more about both of our EMBA programs? Whether you attend in person or online, I look forward to meeting you and helping you find the right fit for your life.
