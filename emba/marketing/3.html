How can you make graduate school fit into your life?

Notre Dame offers you two Executive MBA formats in two different cities — choose one that best fits your schedule and lifestyle. Both allow you to stay home during the week, so you only step away from work and family on the weekends.

The South Bend Cohort:

  • Duration: 21 months
  • Meets one weekend per month (Thursday through Saturday) at the Stayer Center for Executive Education on the campus of Notre Dame
  • Begins in August (August orientation/May graduation)
  • International immersion project in January


If you prefer to complete the EMBA program at an accelerated rate,

The Chicago Cohort:

  • Duration: 17 months
  • Meets every other weekend (Friday and Saturday) in downtown Chicago
  • Begin in January (January orientation/May graduation)
  • 3 weeklong immersions on Notre Dame's campus
  • International immersion project in January

Why not join me for an information session where you can learn more about both of our EMBA programs? Whether you attend in person or online, I am happy to answer your questions.
