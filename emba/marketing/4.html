No two people will have the same experience in the Notre Dame Executive MBA program. But I can assure you of this commonality: Your experience will be profound, life-changing, inspirational and uniquely yours.

  • Learn how to be a different kind of leader on the very first day
    Day one begins with the Executive Integral Leadership (EIL) program. As Susan Jania explains, your first week experience forces you to tackle the important questions like, "what's next for you?" and "what are you going to get out of this?"

  • Connect and forge lifelong bonds with like-minded people
    Your network will grow throughout the duration of your time in the program, beginning with your study team. You then get to know your EMBA class, other EMBA alumni, Mendoza College of Business alumni and Notre Dame alumni. Every step of the way, you will build a network with the right kind of people, leaders who share your values and commitment to business as a force for good.

  • Enjoy a smaller class size for a more unique and personal experience
    "Once you take that first step in, you're a member of the family and it's a very welcoming atmosphere. You can truly lean on people and you truly go above and beyond in terms of what you're willing to do for a fellow classmate that you wouldn't normally do for someone else. It's the shared experience of what we're doing here at Notre Dame that business has a higher purpose."

    • —Jason Holley, ND EMBA '16

Get started with your application, I look forward to answering any of your questions along the way.
