By Practitioners for Practitioners

The University of Notre Dame Mendoza College of Business, Pfizer, Exelon, and Allstate are excited to convene an intimate and private gathering of corporate innovation practitioners to share stories about being a front-line innovators.

Innovation is a tough business and innovation practitioners oftentimes have difficulty finding peers to share the joys and disappointments of being a fron-tline innovator. The Innovation Practitioner Conference held at the University of Notre Dame provides an intimate and private venue where stories can be shared amongst innovation practitioners and where valuable experience can be gained from seasoned veterans of the trade. All for a small investment, that produces immediate on-the-job impact.
