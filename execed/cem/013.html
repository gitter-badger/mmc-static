Under the teaching of our renowned instructors in Notre Dame’s Certificate in Executive Management program, you will gain technical knowledge of subjects such as marketing, finance, accounting and strategic planning. You will also learn about topics vital to every corporate leader: Problem Solving, Leading Change, Crucial Conversations and Negotiation Skills. PLUS, the program will extend your understanding of areas critical to future business success such as Design Thinking, Innovation and Change Management.

WHO SHOULD ATTEND: Managers, executives, and technical experts who want to increase their knowledge of business, management, and leadership.

WHAT YOU WILL GAIN:
• Develop a broader “general management” perspective
• Understand how all business areas contribute to an organization’s success
• Improve functional skills in economics, marketing, accounting and finance
• Expand managerial and leadership skills
• Develop capacity for creative analysis skills and strategic thinking
