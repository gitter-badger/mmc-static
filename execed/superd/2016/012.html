INSPIRE YOUR TEAM TO EXCELLENCE.

April 27-28 or October 18-19, 2016

The Notre Dame Supervisory Development Program can help you develop your toolbox of people management skills. Whether you are new to supervising or are interested in improving your skills, our two-day program will be beneficial. We offer this program two times per year. Register for the April 27-28 (the April 26-27 session has filled up so we opened another session) or October 18-19, 2016 session and experience the beautiful Stayer Center for Executive Education on the Notre Dame campus.

Become current on proven and emerging management principles and techniques. Develop practical supervisory skills that will enable you to analyze, prioritize and delegate. Broaden your perspective to include overall organizational goals and organizational change. Prepare for transitioning into managment and leadership positions. Identify the skill sets of individual employees and develop employees to their fullest potential. Communicate effectively with direct reports, cross-functional teams and superiors.

WHO SHOULD ATTEND: Supervisors, managers and team leaders looking for practical skills for dealing with day-to-day management challenges.

SESSION TOPICS:

  • Management vs. Leadership
  • Optimizing Team Performance
  • Developing Our People Skills
  • Analyzing Performance Problems
  • Leadership Strategies
  • Developing an Action Plan
  • Developing Employees
  • Delegating
  • Understanding Responses to Change
