ND Climate Investing Conference: Climate change through the lens of impact investing

JULY 29, 2015

The Notre Dame Climate Investing Conference, led by the Mendoza College of Business, will bring together researchers, business leaders, investors and environmentalists to explore the changing role and potential impact of investing in regard to climate change. The conference is open to the public and takes place Sept. 29 and 30 at the University of Notre Dame.
