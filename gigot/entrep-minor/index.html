The Gigot Center for Entrepreneurship is announcing a series of exciting enhancements to the Entrepreneurship Minor that will begin rolling out in the Fall 2016 semester. The changes will create more experiential learning opportunities, offer more choice and flexibility, and offer capstone courses in one of three career-aligned tracks of concentration.
