CLIMATE INVESTING CONFERENCE: Transition to a Low-Carbon World

September 29-30, 2015
McKenna Hall, University of Notre Dame

Climate change has been called "the greatest threat to future generations."

But could it also present the greatest opportunity to redefine the very foundations of society to improve the quality of life across the globe AND save the planet?

Does IMPACT INVESTING hold the key?

Join a distinguished gathering of scientists, investors, industry executives, entrepreneurs and policy experts for a discussion about new approaches to solving the urgent challenges of climate change.

Key speakers include:
Fr. John Jenkins, CSC, President of the University of Notre Dame
Roger Huang, Dean of the Mendoza College of Business
David Orr, Professor of Environmental Studies, Oberlin College
Paul Hawken, Project Director, Project Drawdown
Carolyn Woo, President and CEO, Catholic Relief Services
