SPECIAL INVITATION: MENDOZA STUDENTS

KEVIN O’LEARY OF SHARK TANK
5 p.m. – 6:15 p.m.
January 30, 2017
Mendoza College of Business’ Jordan Auditorium

Shark Tank's Kevin "Mr. Wonderful" O'Leary will be on hand to discuss the show, entrepreneurship and the essential elements to develop a successful new business.

As part of the program, three teams of Notre Dame students will pitch their ventures to O'Leary and receive his particular brand of feedback.

Will they survive the Shark Tank? Show up to cheer them on!

DUE TO EXPECTED HEAVY ATTENDANCE …

Doors to the Mendoza College of Business’ Jordan Auditorium will open at 4 p.m. SEATING INSIDE THE JORDAN IS VERY LIMITED. The presentation also will be live streamed to DeBartolo Hall Room 101.

Mendoza students wishing to sit in the Jordan Auditorium MUST:

  1. Reserve a ticket by registering online at the Event Registration Page by January 25, and either printing the ticket or showing the digital code at the door

  2. Arrive at the auditorium between 4 p.m. and 4:30 p.m.

After 4:30 p.m., the auditorium will be opened to ALL guests. Tickets will no longer be honored.

Individuals must be present to claim seats; saving seats will not be allowed.

Don’t miss your chance to support Notre Dame students as they go head-to- head with Mr. Wonderful in the Shark Tank!
