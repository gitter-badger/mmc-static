Happy New Year!

If you made resolutions for 2016, you’re not alone. In fact, about 45 percent of us make New Year’s resolutions, and 47 percent of those resolutions involve self-improvement through education.

That’s where Notre Dame’s Master of Nonprofit Administration can help. Whether you are transitioning into the workforce after volunteering or performing service work, or you’re already in the nonprofit sector and are looking to do more with your career, the Notre Dame MNA program is a perfect way to better yourself, your career and your community.

Here’s how to get started:

  1. VISIT THE MASTER OF NONPROFIT ADMINISTRATION WEBSITE to learn more about the program and to determine whether it’s a good fit for you (the program is flexible and allows most students to maintain full-time employment).

  2. REQUEST A VIEWBOOK which not only includes more details about the program, but it also features real students in real classes—these are your peers. Can you visualize yourself in their seats?

  3. REGISTER FOR OUR ONLINE INFORMATION SESSION, which takes place on Tuesday, Jan. 26, 2016 from 6-7 p.m. EST. During the presentation, you may pose questions to MNA staff members as well as webinar guests.

  4. APPLY BY THE LAST DEADLINE: FEBRUARY 15, 2016 and get ready to educate both your servant heart and your business mind.
