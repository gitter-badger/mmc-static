There exists a short list of the best Master of Science in Accountancy (MSA) and Master of Accounting (MACC) programs in the country. The MSA program at Notre Dame is on that list every year, and is known for its quality, rigor and focus on ethics.

One academic year—that's two semesters and 30 total credit hours—is all it takes to receive a degree from a top-4 MSA program and change the trajectory of your career forever. And you’ll still have time for an interterm study abroad opportunity in China or South America for two weeks.

Whether you’re interested in public accounting, corporate finance, or working for a government agency, there’s a track for you in the MSA program at Notre Dame.
