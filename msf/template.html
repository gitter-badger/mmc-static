We know that deciding to invest your time and money into education is not easy. As the saying goes, if it were, everyone would do it.

Earning your Notre Dame Executive MBA isn't easy and is not for everyone, nor should it be. Fortunately, applying for the program is easy.

For first round eligibility for fellowships and to have your admission decision by April 30, take a few minutes to start your application by April 1. Don't worry, you'll have a few weeks after that to submit all of your materials.

I would also like to invite you to join us for an information session on April 5 on campus at the Stayer Center for Executive Education. This will be a great opportunity to speak with members of the EMBA team, as well as alumni of the program. We're confident your experience here will solidify your decision. Please visit executiveMBA.nd.edu to register.

Please contact me if you have any questions and let me know if there's any way I can help you in this process.
