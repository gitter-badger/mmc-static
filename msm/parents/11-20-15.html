I offer my sincere best wishes as your senior plans for life after graduation. This is an exciting time, but we know that for some students, it’s also a time of uncertainty. Many are still trying to decide where to apply their knowledge and talents.

The Notre Dame Master of Science in Management is designed for students in this very moment in their lives. This 10-month degree program combines one-on-one career development with the fundamentals of business. We help each student build an individual brand that combines his or her undergraduate non-business major with accounting, finance, marketing, and management skills.

An English major may choose to create powerful marketing campaigns. An engineer can combine technical and business knowledge to advance into project management. A math major could learn to translate data into insight for a major corporation. The possibilities are limitless.

Over the next several months, there will be many opportunities to learn more. We offer open office hours and information sessions for students, and even webinars for parents like you. GRE or GMAT scores are required, so it’s important that applicants get started right away. I invite you to learn more at mendoza.nd.edu/MSM.

In Notre Dame,
