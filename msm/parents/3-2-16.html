As your child gets ready to enter this challenging job market, I wanted to let you know about a graduate business degree program that was specifically designed for graduating students who majored in something other than business, and who are looking for opportunities to further enhance their marketability.

The Notre Dame Master of Science in Management (MSM) is a 10-month degree program, designed to maximize your child's non-business undergraduate experience (English, Engineering, Science, etc.) by adding relevant business knowledge and skills. The MSM is a pre-experience program, which means students enter it directly after completing their undergraduate degrees.

The MSM program provides foundational knowledge in key areas such as finance, accounting, marketing and management, as well as focused job-seeking skills development. The result is a graduate prepared to pursue a career he or she deeply cares about, who is armed with the business acumen vital to long-term success.

I invite you to learn more by watching this video interview with one of our recent Notre Dame MSM graduates, Ryan, and his mother, Susan Dominguez.

Applications for the upcoming 2016-2017 class are being accepted on a rolling basis until the cohort is filled or until May 1.
